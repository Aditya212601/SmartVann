Why Analyze Population Density?

Understanding population density helps in identifying overpopulated regions, assessing air quality risk zones, planning sustainable development, and exploring future opportunities for urban management.

Key Features

  • Identify Overpopulated Regions: Highlight areas with critical population density.
  • Assess Risk Zones for Air Quality: Evaluate areas with poor AQI and high health risks.
  • Plan Sustainable Development: Use density data for resource allocation and urban planning.
  • Explore New Opportunities: Investigate innovative ways to manage high-density regions.

Latest air quality index (AQI) data shows critical hotspots in urban regions, with values ranging from 50 (moderate) to over 300 (hazardous).
